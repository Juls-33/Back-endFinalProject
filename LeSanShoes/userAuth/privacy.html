Lesanshoes Privacy Policy

This privacy policy ("policy") explains how Lesanshoes ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you visit and use Lesanshoes.com ("website", "service").

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time, and we will notify you of any significant changes. To stay informed about the latest updates, we encourage you to review this policy regularly

What User Data We Collect

When you visit Lesanshoes.com, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, contact number, and physical address.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and web pages visited on our site.
  • Order Information: Details of the products you purchase, your payment details (for transactions), and shipping information.

Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your data for several reasons:

  • To provide and improve our services: We use your information to process orders, deliver products, and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your shopping experience: We analyze your behavior on our website to offer personalized product recommendations and content that suits your preferences.
  • To communicate with you: We may send you order updates, promotional emails, and newsletters with information we believe may interest you (you can opt out of marketing communications at any time).
  • To improve our website: We use your data to enhance the functionality and performance of our site.
  • To comply with legal obligations: We may use your data for legal purposes if required by law.

How We Safeguard Your Data

Lesanshoes is committed to protecting your personal data. We implement the latest encryption technology and best practices to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure of your information.

We regularly monitor our systems to ensure your data remains secure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

Cookie Policy

When you visit our website, we may use cookies to collect data about your online behavior, such as:

  • The pages you visit
  • How much time you spend on each page
  • Your preferences and interest

This helps us customize the website and improve your shopping experience. You can control the use of cookies by adjusting your browser settings. For detailed instructions on how to disable cookies, visit www.internetcookies.com.

Please note that cookies do not allow us to control your device; they are only used to gather data that helps improve our website.

Restricting the Collection of Your Personal Data

If you wish to limit the collection or use of your personal information, you can do the following:

  • During checkout or registration, ensure that you uncheck any boxes that request additional marketing or promotional information.
  • If you have previously agreed to share your information and wish to change this, please contact us via email, and we will update your preferences.

Sharing Your Data

Lesanshoes will not sell, lease, or distribute your personal information to third parties without your consent. We may share your data with trusted service providers for purposes like payment processing, shipping, or marketing (if you have opted into those services).

We may also share your information when required by law or in response to legal proceedings.

Your Rights

As a user of Lesanshoes, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request correctiong to your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent for marketing communications at any time.

To exercise these rights or for any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at customersupport@Lesanshoes.com or through the contact form on our website.

By using Lesanshoes.com, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please refrain from using our services.
